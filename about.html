Our Story

At JAV Coaching, we understand the unique challenges South Asians face when it comes to fitness and health — from busy family lives to cultural food traditions. Our mission is to bridge the gap between modern training science and South Asian lifestyles.

Founded by Arjav Chhabra, a certified personal trainer with roots in the South Asian community, JAV Coaching blends expertise with cultural understanding to help clients achieve sustainable results.

Coach's Philosophy & Approach

We don't believe in one-size-fits-all solutions or quick fixes that don't last. Our approach is built on three core principles that set us apart from traditional fitness programs.

Sustainable Results Over Quick Fixes

We focus on building habits and lifestyle changes that you can maintain long-term. No extreme diets or impossible workout schedules that leave you burned out after a few weeks.

Cultural Food Integration

We understand that South Asian cuisine is rich in flavor and tradition. Our approach helps you maintain cultural food connections while building sustainable eating habits that support your fitness goals.

Holistic Wellness

Fitness isn't just about the gym. We address stress management, sleep quality, mental health, and accountability to create lasting change in all areas of your life.

Our Client-Centered Values

Inclusivity

All ages, genders, and fitness levels are welcome. Whether you're a busy parent, a college student, or a grandparent wanting to stay active, we create programs that work for you.

Community

We build support systems, not just workouts. Our clients become part of a community that understands the unique challenges of balancing South Asian culture with health goals.

Personalization

No cookie-cutter plans. Every program is designed specifically for your schedule, preferences, cultural background, and personal goals.

Our Personal Journeys

The stories behind why we do what we do

Arjav's Story

At just 16 years old, Arjav began his fitness journey and successfully lost 60 pounds. Behind that transformation came challenges: extreme dieting and the struggles of an eating disorder. Determined to find a healthier way, he immersed himself in fitness and nutrition research, which sparked a lifelong passion for bodybuilding and wellness.

After completing his NASM certification, Arjav began by training clients in person, channeling his personal experiences into helping others avoid the same pitfalls. In 2022, he founded JAV Coaching with a clear mission: to tackle the very struggles he once faced and empower others to achieve lasting results through sustainable fitness and nutrition.

Bhargava's Story

Bhargava began his fitness journey in high school after years of struggling with being severely underweight. Born with multiple food allergies, he was forced to adapt to strict dietary restrictions while finding ways to build muscle, gain weight, and create a sustainable healthy lifestyle. Over time, this challenge fueled his passion for bodybuilding, health, and overall wellness.

Since then, Bhargava has continued to pursue his own wellness goals while coaching and supporting individuals from diverse backgrounds and lifestyles. In partnership with Arjav, he has guided clients through weight loss, muscle gain, and long-term habit building—helping them prioritize wellness, improve confidence, and embrace healthier lifestyles in every aspect of their lives.
